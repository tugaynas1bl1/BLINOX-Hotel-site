Tugay Nasibli

Hi, my name is Tugay Nasibli. I'm a founder of blinox hotel project. My occupation is programming and when I was a 15 year old teenager, I used to go to programming academy 'STEP IT Academy'. My teacher gave us a task for creating a startup project. I overthought about it and found an idea about an hotel. I created a 3D design, made a presentation and my teacher loved it. A few years later I made this project come true. You know God created one person for another person. God created a person to be another person's hope. Unfortunately in this desperate world people suffer from various things. All of us are human. I thought, if I can see, then why other people have to be not ablr to see the perfections created by God. All over the world the blind come to our hotel. They are so private, too precious.I became their hope, and it is your turn, who is reading it. BECOME THEIR HOPE IN THIS DESPERATE WORLD.
